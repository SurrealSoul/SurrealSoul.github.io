Super Lizard

The super lizard, otherwise known as Mr.Slurps, grew up with humble beginnings in a small town, growing up with his mother, brother, and father.


But one day, a flood washed him away from his village and family. He found himself struggling for survival in the depths of the Pacific ocean. Eventually, he passed out. He woke in a cave on the land. He heard noises echoing around him. Then he heard voices in his head telling him his destiny was to destroy the crystals inside golem. For some reason he understood. So he set off, to defeat the golem. Along the way he felt power and might rush into him.

Ten months later he had golem laying in crumbling pieces at his feet. Golem was no match for the Super Lizard. He reins victorios as a hero for all!
